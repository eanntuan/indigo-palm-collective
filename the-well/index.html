The Well

Palm Springs, California

Where minimalism feels like a hug.

Settle In. Clear Your Head. Leave Feeling Well.

The Well is a 1BR wellness retreat in Palm Springs built for people who need a real reset. Clean design, smart storage, and a layout that doesn't require you to think. You arrive, unpack, and the space does the rest.

Southwestern minimalism meets mid-century modern: Navajo pattern work, meditative line motifs, quality pieces chosen to stand out rather than fill space. Not a budget rental dressed up. The kind of place you'd actually want to have people over.

One bedroom, sleeps two. The kitchen is fully stocked for real home cooking. A local restaurant guide doubles as a desert cookbook. Smart storage keeps everything organized. The outdoor space works for slow mornings or sharing a meal under the desert sky.

Location & Getting Around

The Well sits in Palm Springs proper, walkable to downtown. Palm Canyon Drive is a short walk. The Uptown Design District, weekend farmers market, and the spa scene are all close. You can skip the car for most of it.

Tahquitz Canyon trail: under 10 minutes. Palm Springs Aerial Tramway: under 15. Indian Wells Tennis Garden (BNP Paribas Open): 25 minutes east. Joshua Tree National Park: under an hour.

Who Stays Here

Solo travelers who need real alone time. Couples who want intimacy over square footage. Remote workers who think better in quiet, well-designed environments. People mid-transition: a big decision to make, a creative project stuck in the noise, something to reconnect with. The Well draws guests who know the difference between a trip that recharges you and one that doesn't.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking The Well.

What makes The Well different from other Palm Springs vacation rentals? +

The Well is designed for people who need actual mental quiet. Sage green palette, smart storage for longer stays, a dedicated remote work setup, and deliberate minimalism that gives you room to breathe. For solo travelers, couples, and remote workers who are sensitive to visual overwhelm.

Is The Well good for remote work and extended stays? +

Yes. Dedicated workspace, high-speed WiFi, smart storage for clothes and gear, and a calm environment that supports focus. Designed for 30+ day mid-term stays and remote workers who want to work from somewhere beautiful.

How close is The Well to downtown Palm Springs? +

Central Palm Springs. Downtown restaurants, coffee shops, and hiking trails are close. Most of what makes Palm Springs worth visiting is walkable or a short drive away.

How many guests does The Well sleep? +

Up to 4 guests in 1 bedroom. Ideal for solo travelers, couples, or two friends who want a calm, design-forward base in Palm Springs.

How much do I save booking directly? +

You skip the platform fees entirely. Airbnb and VRBO charge guests 14-20% on top of the nightly rate. Booking direct means no service fees, no booking charges.
