## Instagram Strategy

### Content Pillars
1. **Property Showcases** (40%) - Hero shots of each property
2. **Design Details** (30%) - Close-ups that tell the story
3. **Guest Experience** (20%) - Amenities, welcome details, thoughtful touches
4. **Brand Story** (10%) - Behind-the-scenes, philosophy, host insights

**Instagram**: 4-5 posts/week
- Monday: Property Showcase
- Wednesday: Design Detail
- Friday: Guest Experience/Amenity
- Weekend: Carousel or Brand Story

## Photo Selection Guide

### Best Performers on Pinterest:
1. **Pool shots** (especially with unique details like Terra Luz's vintage lounger/tile)
2. **Wide living room shots** (showcase full space)
3. **Bathroom details** (tile work, fixtures)
4. **Outdoor/patio areas** (lifestyle aspirational)
5. **Kitchen setups** (practical + beautiful)

### Best Performers on Instagram:
1. **Hero property shots** (exterior/pool)
2. **Moody interior details** (close-ups with depth)
3. **Unexpected amenities** (coffee setup, straw visors, design details)
4. **Before/after transformations** (if you have any)
5. **Carousel walkthroughs** (multiple room showcase)

**Instagram**:
- Engagement rate (likes + comments / followers)

## Pro Tips

1. **Watermark Pinterest images subtly** with "Indigo Palm Collective" so they're traceable
2. **Create Pinterest boards** for each property + "Desert Design Inspiration" + "Palm Springs Travel Guide"
3. **Use Instagram carousels** for highest engagement (10x more than single images)
4. **Post Instagram at optimal times**: 9-11am PST, 7-9pm PST
5. **Pinterest pins live forever** - create evergreen content that works year-round
6. **Repurpose content**: Same photo → Pinterest pin → Instagram post → Instagram Story
7. **Engage with similar accounts**: Like/comment on #palmspringsairbnb #desertvacation posts to build community

---

## Content Batching Workflow

**Monthly (2 hours)**:
1. Select 20-30 photos (5-8 per property)
2. Write all captions using templates above
3. Schedule in Later/Planoly/Buffer

**Weekly (30 mins)**:
1. Post scheduled content
2. Respond to comments/DMs
3. Check analytics and adjust
