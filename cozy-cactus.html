Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking Cozy Cactus.

How far is Cozy Cactus from Coachella and Stagecoach? +

2.5 miles from Empire Polo Club — about 8–10 minutes by car. You can also walk: 1.5 miles to the festival gate via Eisenhower Drive (~19 min). Golf cart rides within Indian Palms are available from community members during festival weekends.

Is Cozy Cactus good for families with young children? +

It's designed specifically for families. Pack-n-play, sound machines, Stokke high chair, curated toy collection, art supplies, pool safety gate, and a complete labeling system so nothing gets lost. Parents describe it as "the first vacation where I actually rested."

Does Cozy Cactus have a heated pool and hot tub? +

Yes. Heated private pool and hot tub with pool lighting for evening swims. The pool deck has lounge chairs, outdoor dining, a fire pit, and a putting green. Pool heat is available for an additional fee.

Are pets allowed at Cozy Cactus? +

Pets are not permitted at Cozy Cactus. For a pet-friendly option, Terra Luz in Indio welcomes dogs with advance approval — contact indigopalmco@gmail.com.

How much do I save booking directly? +

15% versus Airbnb or VRBO. Airbnb charges 15–20% in platform fees — we pass those savings back to you. No service fees, no booking charges.
