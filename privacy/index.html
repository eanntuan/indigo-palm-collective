Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This privacy policy applies to Indigo Palm Collective and its associated tools, including the indigopalm.co website and any integrations used to manage content publishing.

What We Collect

If you submit your email address through our newsletter signup form, we store that email to send you occasional updates about our properties and the Coachella Valley. We do not sell or share your email with third parties.

Our website uses Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymous usage data including pages visited, time on site, and general location (city/region level). No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

We use Microsoft Clarity for session recording and heatmaps. This data is anonymous and used solely to improve the website experience.

We use the Pinterest Tag to measure the effectiveness of our Pinterest content. This tag collects anonymous engagement data.

Pinterest Integration

Indigo Palm Collective uses the Pinterest API to publish pins to our Pinterest account (@indigopalmco) automatically. This integration accesses only our own Pinterest account and does not collect, store, or process any data from Pinterest users. No user data from Pinterest is retained by this application.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies set by Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, and Pinterest for the purposes described above. These are standard analytics and tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services on this website: Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, Pinterest Tag, and Resend for email delivery. Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.
