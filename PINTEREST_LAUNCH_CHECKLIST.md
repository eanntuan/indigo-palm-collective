## 🚨 TROUBLESHOOTING

### "I don't see Magic Design option"
- Your template isn't marked as "Brand Template" yet
- Go to Brand Kit → Templates → Toggle "Brand Template" ON
- Refresh Canva

### "Content Planner won't connect to Pinterest"
- Make sure you have Pinterest Business account (not personal)
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting
- Check Pinterest Settings → Apps → Authorize Canva

### "My pins look blurry"
- Upload higher resolution photos (1000px minimum width)
- Check file size isn't over 20MB (Canva limit)
- Use PNG for graphics, JPG for photos

### "Bulk Create isn't working with CSV"
- Save as CSV (not Excel .xlsx)
- Check column headers match exactly: property_name, headline, description
- Remove special characters (smart quotes, em dashes)

### "How do I know which headlines work best?"
- Wait 30 days for data
- Pinterest Analytics → Pins → Sort by "Outbound Clicks"
- Double down on winner styles next month
