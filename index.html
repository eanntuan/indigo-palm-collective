Yes — and it's one of the best parts about staying here. You have the BEST location to the festival with no long shuttle lines or crazy surge pricing. From the property, it's about a 0.7 mile walk within Indian Palms to the front security gate, then an additional 0.3 miles to the Yellow Path at 49th & Monroe. Total walk is about 1.5 miles via Eisenhower Dr (~19 min). Bring comfy shoes. The path is gravel. No pedestrians are allowed on Monroe (between Ave 49–52) or Madison (between Ave 50–52).

Yes — community members within Indian Palms often offer golf cart rides or "tailgate taxis" to the front gate for a few dollars. The going rate is roughly $5/rider for a group of 10, or $10/rider for smaller groups. We recommend planning this in advance. It saves your feet for the festival. We'll include current contact info in your welcome book closer to your stay.

Avoid driving if at all possible during festival hours. Traffic around 48th & Monroe gets completely gridlocked. If you must drive, use the Monroe Gate (main guest entry, the only gate with security staff). Do not use the Avenue 50 Gate: it's restricted to residents only and you will be turned around. During festival weekends, Avenue 50 between Madison & Monroe experiences full road closures Friday through Sunday, along with several surrounding streets. We'll share the full road closure map in your welcome book.

Indian Palms Country Club has plenty to keep you busy between sets. The community offers 3 pools, pickleball courts (we provide paddles), and tennis courts (ID required to rent rackets). There's also a Lifestyle Center fitness facility. Let us know in advance if you'd like access and we'll get you set up.
