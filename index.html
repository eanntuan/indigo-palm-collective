About Indigo Palm Collective

United by a shared emotional current, these homes are designed to restore and reenergize travelers.

Where many getaways are designed to help travelers disconnect, Indigo Palm Collective creates desert spaces meant for playful reconnection. Each stay is thoughtfully curated to reawaken presence, creativity, and a sense of belonging—places that feel alive yet calming, expressive yet effortless. United by a shared emotional current, these homes are designed to restore and reenergize travelers in a way that feels inspired yet natural, not prescribed.

No two properties are the same, yet each carries a distinct emotional warmth that is both inviting and lively. Rooted in high-touch hospitality that is both intuitive and intentional, Indigo Palm Collective blends story, style, and human connection, creating spaces that feel personal, considered, and quietly transformative.

"Our mission is to create experiential stays that ignite inner energy through outward care."

Indigo, by nature, represents depth of energy: intuition and heightened awareness. Long associated with creativity and emotional balance, it lives in the space between blue and violet—calm yet charged, grounding yet expansive.

Palm reflects a fully alive, vibrant symbol of renewal against a dry landscape. Like a palm tree rising with quiet confidence from the desert floor, our homes restore energy through thoughtful design, interactive amenities, and a deep sense of hospitality where connective roots form and travelers feel held in their desert getaway.

About Your Host

Eann's journey into hospitality began at a crossroads: invest in a Tesla—or invest in something that lit her up. She chose hospitality, channeling her energy into her first property, The Cozy Cactus in Indio, and hasn't looked back since. A surfer from Culver City with a bright, bubbly spirit and an instinct for care, Eann brings both ease and intention into every home she creates. Her spaces reflect her personality: relaxed but dialed in, playful yet thoughtfully considered.

What truly excites Eann about hosting is the moment guests realize everything's already been thought of. From sharp knives and ZipLoc bag organizers to great coffee, blackout curtains, and even the occasional unexpected delight (like the straw visors), she designs with empathy—anticipating needs before guests ever have to ask.

She understands travel from the inside out: the desire to arrive, exhale, and feel instantly at home without unpacking your whole life. For Eann, hosting isn't about perfection, it's about presence. Making guests feel seen, cared for, and genuinely comfortable is the through-line in every stay she offers.
