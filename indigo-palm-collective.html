About Indigo Palm Collective

Four homes. One host. Zero corporate amenities.

I was at a crossroads: invest in a Tesla, or something that actually lit me up. I chose the houses.

What started as one vacation rental in Indio has grown into a collection of four, each one designed with a distinct personality and a specific guest in mind. The Cozy Cactus for families who need a real reset. Casa Moto for gatherings and the four-legged. The Well for solo travelers who need to think. The Sundune for wanderers who don't.

Every property is designed by The Olive Jar and built around one idea: hosting isn't about perfection. It's about presence. Anticipating what you need before you think to ask for it.

"Surfing isn't about chasing perfect waves. It's about recognizing the simple wonder of it all."

Same goes for hosting. Every property in this collection is designed around the specific kind of guest who belongs in it: their pace, their aesthetic, what they need at 7am and what they need at midnight. Nothing is accidental. Nothing is generic.

Designed by The Olive Jar. Hosted by someone who just really loves a good house.
