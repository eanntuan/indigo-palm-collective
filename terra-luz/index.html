A Sun-Washed Desert Sanctuary

Terra Luz is a vibrant Indio cabana retreat blending Old Havana character and desert warmth — with a one-of-a-kind, color-drenched pool and patio made for slow, sun-soaked days. A sensory sanctuary where the pace instinctively slows and everything moves to an unspoken rhythm — steady and warm, like a conga echoing in the background.

With Cuban character woven throughout, Terra Luz is full of vibrant color and culture reflective of wabi-sabi warmth and the patina of Old Havana. Spaces that invite exhale and connection, indoors and out — calm yet alive with personality, color, and culture. The Frida-blue pool is exactly as dramatic as it sounds. The Kitchen Terrace — a sun-soaked nook framed by a sliding pass-through window overlooking the pool — is where morning coffee becomes mimosas and mimosas become afternoon pool chats. Restorative, radiant, and unmistakably alive.

Designed for groups and families who travel with intention. Where sun-soaked afternoons, shared meals from the Kitchen Terrace, and easy togetherness matter more than rigid itineraries. The real luxury here is permission to slow down — vibrant without being loud, rejuvenating without requiring effort.

Three bedrooms, two bathrooms, sleeps up to six. The primary suite faces the pool. The kitchen is fully stocked — not just technically, but actually. Outdoor dining under the desert sky. Patterned robes waiting in the closet. Cuban roasted coffee beans on the counter. Mexican dominos on the shelf. This is the vacation rental that makes you wonder why you ever booked a hotel.

Location & Getting Around

Terra Luz sits inside Indian Palms Country Club in Indio, California — a gated neighborhood with golf courses, tennis courts, and a community pool steps away. Just 2.5 miles from Empire Polo Club, home of the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival and Stagecoach Country Music Festival. That's 8–10 minutes by car. No shuttle lines, no shuttle stress — one of the closest vacation rentals to the festival grounds in the entire Coachella Valley.

Downtown Indio is five minutes away. L&G Desert Store is literally around the corner. Papa Headz for late-night bites, Gambino's Crepes for slow mornings, Everbloom for matcha, the Pink Cabana at The Sands Hotel in Indian Wells for a proper cocktail, La Quinta Cliffhouse for views. Joshua Tree National Park is 45 minutes east. Palm Springs is 30 minutes west. Indian Wells Tennis Garden (BNP Paribas Open) is 20 minutes away.

Who Stays Here

Couples who want a private pool without a hotel. Friend groups hitting Coachella or Stagecoach who refuse to split a hotel hallway. Families who need a real kitchen and real outdoor space. Design-obsessed travelers who find generic vacation rentals genuinely depressing. Anyone who's been burned by photos that lie — the pool is exactly as saturated as it looks. Terra Luz is exactly that warm in person. It's the real thing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking Terra Luz.

Is Terra Luz pet-friendly? +

Yes. Terra Luz welcomes dogs with advance approval. Contact us at indigopalmco@gmail.com for pet policy details and any applicable fees. We ask that pets stay off furniture and are never left unattended.

How far is Terra Luz from Coachella and Stagecoach? +

2.5 miles from Empire Polo Club — about 8–10 minutes by car. One of the closest vacation rentals to the festival grounds in the entire Coachella Valley.

What makes Terra Luz unique? +

It's a Latin/Cuban-inspired sanctuary designed by brand architect Dawn Asher. Kahlo blue saltwater pool, terracotta courtyard, wabi-sabi warmth throughout — an aesthetic that feels collected over time rather than staged. The Coachella Valley's only vacation rental with a cohesive Old Havana design identity.

How many guests does Terra Luz sleep? +

Up to 6 guests in 3 bedrooms. Full kitchen, living and dining areas, private saltwater pool, and outdoor entertaining space included.

How much do I save booking directly? +

You skip the platform fees entirely. Airbnb and VRBO charge guests 14-20% on top of the nightly rate. Booking direct means no service fees, no booking charges.
