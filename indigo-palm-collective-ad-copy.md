## 🎯 Why This Works Better

### More Inventory = Higher Conversion
- ❌ Single property: Limited dates available
- ✅ 4 properties: Something available almost always

### Self-Selection
- Cozy Cactus: Couples, retro lovers
- Casa Moto: Families, mid-week stays
- PS Retreat: Festival-goers, modern design fans
- The Well: Large groups (when available)

### Stronger Value Proposition
- "Choose from 4 properties" > "Here's 1 property"
- Feels like curated collection (premium)
- More likely to find dates that work
