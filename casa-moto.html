Casa Moto

Indian Palms Country Club, California

For travelers tired of beige Airbnbs.

Where Cuban Warmth Meets Desert Cool

Most desert rentals look the same. Beige walls, the same succulents, generic "boho" from a catalog. Casa Moto doesn't.

Hand-tiled pool with Cuban mosaic details. Vintage yellow lounger. Heart carved into the fence. Design that transports you, not just houses you. This is where Latin soul meets Coachella Valley, where every detail tells a story, and where color and warmth cocoon you while the desert stretches beyond.

Perfect for groups who want to unwind together in a space that celebrates connection, warmth, and the laid-back luxury of Coachella Valley living.
