Getting Here

Arrival

Check-in is at 4 pm. If you're early, head to Cartel Coffee on Palm Canyon and grab a pastry.
  • 1

    Door Code

    Your code arrives at least 24 hours before check-in. It works from 4 pm on arrival day through 11 am on checkout day.

  • 2

    Driving In

    GPS sometimes routes to Highway 111 instead of the lot. Turn at the Golf Club Drive traffic light to enter Palm Canyon Villas.

  • 3

    Parking

    Enter Lot 5 and find Carport #555. Guest spaces G572–578 are also in Lot 5 if you have a second car. Lot 4 has guest parking too and is slightly closer to the unit. Waverly Dr has street parking as a backup — I've never seen all the spots taken.

  • 4

    Walk to the Unit (~5 min)

    Walk toward the dumpsters and take the leftmost path into the community. Follow the signs until you see 184–187 (about 150 ft), then make a right. Go up the stairs — the unit is on the second floor on your left.

  • 5

    Entering the Condo

    Open the black screen door (it's unlocked). Enter your 4-digit code on the blue Schlage keypad. If the keypad is dark, press the bottom left corner to wake it — don't tap anywhere else first, Schlage counts any touch as a keystroke. You may need to pull the door handle toward you to align the lock.

Things to Do

Palm Springs Tram

Climb 6,000 ft to San Jacinto Peak. Plus it's next door.

Moorten Botanical Garden

Cacti and succulents you won't see anywhere else. Good for an hour.

Modernism Week

If you're visiting in February, rearrange your schedule for this.

Coachella

Two weekends in April, the valley becomes something else entirely.

Stagecoach

More into country? (Me too). Grab your boots and bandana.

Art Museum

Desert art, European works, Native American collections. Thursdays are free.

The V Pool Party

Weekends at The Sonder Hotel. Palm Springs pool party vibe.

Vintage Market

MCM finds, first Sunday of the month, October–May. Go early.

El Paseo

Open-air designer shopping in Palm Desert with mountain views. Closer than it looks.
