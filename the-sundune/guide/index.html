Eat & Drink

Where to Eat in Palm Springs

Palm Springs has a small-city food scene that punches above its weight. Most of these are within 10 minutes of The Sundune.

Coffee
Cartel Roasting Co.
Specialty Coffee
Hip coffeehouse with excellent cold brew. The one the welcome guide recommends stocking in the fridge.
1551 N Palm Canyon Dr • cartelroasting.co
Koffi
Specialty Coffee
Great cold brew and an excellent banana walnut muffin. Outdoor patio, worth the stop.
650 E Tahquitz Canyon Way • kofficoffee.com
Breakfast & Casual
King's Highway
Cal-Mexican Diner
Lively diner with a Cal-Mexican menu inside the Ace Hotel. Good for a late breakfast or early lunch.
701 E Palm Canyon Dr • kingshighwaydiner.com
The Heyday
Diner
Retro diner with standout smashburgers. Fun casual spot for a quick meal.
1550 N Palm Canyon Dr • theheydaypalmsprings.com
Sherman's Deli & Bakery
NYC-Style Deli
A Palm Springs institution. Classic deli sandwiches, towering portions, proper pickle on the side.
401 E Tahquitz Canyon Way • shermansdeli.com
Palm Greens Cafe
Health-Conscious
Plant-forward menu for when you want something lighter between pool sessions.
611 S Palm Canyon Dr #6 • palmgreenscafe.com
Dinner & Drinks
La Perlita Mexican Food
Mexican
Traditional Mexican with strong margaritas. Close to The Sundune, local crowd, no-frills.
901 Crossley Rd • 760-778-8014
Las Palmas Brewing
Brewery & Wine Bar
Beer, wine, and tacos on a lively patio. Good spot before walking Palm Canyon.
461 N Palm Canyon Dr • laspalmasbrewing.com
533 Viet Fusion
Vietnamese & Cocktails
Vietnamese dishes with a cocktail lounge feel. Good dinner option on the south end of town.
1775 E Palm Canyon Dr STE 625
The Nest
Live Music & Dining
Live music, dancing, drinks, and food in Indian Wells. A real night out. Worth the 20-minute drive.
75188 CA-111, Indian Wells • gotothenest.com
Dessert
Over the Rainbow Desserts
Cupcakes & Baked Goods
Cupcakes, cookies, and baked goods. One of those places you go once and end up back twice.
1775 E Palm Canyon Dr STE 150 • overtherainbowdesserts.com
Shopping
El Paseo Shopping District
Outdoor Shopping
Palm Desert's answer to Rodeo Drive. Global designers, indie boutiques, and the famous Wilma & Frieda pop tarts. 20 minutes out.
El Paseo, Palm Desert

Things to Do Near Palm Springs

The desert around Palm Springs is the real draw. Museums, trails, and things that surprise you. Hours change seasonally so call ahead.

Culture & Museums
Palm Springs Art Museum
Art Museum
Strong permanent and rotating collections. Free Thursday afternoons, which is genuinely good value.
101 Museum Dr • psmuseum.org
Palm Springs Air Museum
Aviation Museum
Impressive WWII-era aircraft collection with a working flight simulator. Better than you expect.
745 N Gene Autry Trail • palmspringsairmuseum.org
Living Desert Zoo & Gardens
Zoo & Botanical Garden
Desert ecosystem with animal exhibits and stunning botanical gardens. Go early before the heat picks up.
47900 Portola Ave, Palm Desert • livingdesert.org
Outdoor & Active
Bear Creek Trailhead
Hiking
Nature trail in La Quinta with rocky terrain and mountain views. Worth the 35-minute drive.
La Quinta, CA • Check trail conditions before going
Cabazon Dinosaurs
Roadside Attraction
Giant concrete dinosaurs off the 10 freeway. On the way to or from LA, it is 100% worth 20 minutes.
50770 Seminole Dr, Cabazon • cabazondinosaurs.com
Entertainment
Escape Room Palm Springs
Escape Room
The largest escape room in Southern California. Good option for a group night or a hot afternoon inside.
2500 N Palm Canyon Dr B3 • escapeps.com
Shots in the Night
Mini-Golf
Glow-in-the-dark mini-golf with lasers and music at Indian Wells Golf Resort. Unexpectedly fun.
43-500 Monterey Ave, Palm Desert • Book tickets
Ice Skating at Acrisure Arena
Ice Skating
Yes, you can ice skate in the desert. Acrisure Arena in Palm Desert has public sessions year-round.
75702 Varner Rd, Palm Desert • Book sessions
The Shops at Palm Desert
Indoor Mall
Air-conditioned indoor mall when it gets too hot outside. Target, Macy's, and a solid food court.
72-840 CA-111, Palm Desert • shopsatpalmdesert.com
