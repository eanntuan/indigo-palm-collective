# Google Business Profile Setup Guide

## Why This Helps
When someone Googles "vacation rental Indio" or "Airbnb near Coachella," your property can appear in Google Maps and search results - completely independent of Airbnb's algorithm.

## Casa Moto - Priority Setup

### Step 1: Create/Claim Your Listing

1. Go to: https://business.google.com
2. Click **Add your business**
3. Enter business name: **Casa Moto - Luxury Vacation Rental**
4. Choose category: **Vacation home rental** (primary)
   - Add secondary: **Holiday accommodation service**
5. Enter address: [Your Casa Moto property address]
6. Check: "Yes, I provide goods and services to customers at this address"

### Step 2: Verify Ownership

Google will offer verification methods:
- **Postcard** (most common): Takes 5-7 days, mailed to property
- **Phone call**: If available for your address
- **Email**: If you have domain email
- **Instant verification**: If property already linked to Search Console

**Recommendation**: Use postcard verification - most reliable for vacation rentals

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile (Critical for Visibility)

#### Business Information
- **Business name**: Casa Moto - Luxury Vacation Rental
- **Category**: Vacation home rental
- **Address**: [Full property address]
- **Service area**: Indio, Coachella Valley, Palm Springs area
- **Phone**: Your booking inquiry number
- **Website**: https://indigopalm.co/casa-moto.html

#### Business Hours
Since you're 24/7 bookable online:
- Select: "Open 24 hours" OR
- Add note: "Book online anytime at indigopalm.co"

#### Attributes (Show all that apply)
- ✅ Wi-Fi available
- ✅ Pool
- ✅ Parking available
- ✅ Air conditioning
- ✅ Pet-friendly [if applicable]
- ✅ Family-friendly
- ✅ Wheelchair accessible [if applicable]

### Step 4: Add Photos (High Priority)

Google prioritizes businesses with quality photos. Upload:

**Required (minimum 10 photos)**:
1. Exterior/street view
2. Pool area (day)
3. Pool area (night/lit)
4. Living room
5. Primary bedroom
6. Kitchen
7. Outdoor seating area
8. Bathroom
9. Neighborhood/location shots
10. Unique features (Cuban/Latin decor elements)

**Photo specs**:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: Between 10KB and 5MB
- Resolution: 720px tall, 720px wide minimum
- Quality: High resolution, well-lit, no filters

**Pro tip**: Use your best Airbnb photos, but add unique angles Google doesn't have yet

### Step 5: Write Your Description (350 characters max)

```

Private heated pool, 3BR/2BA, chef's kitchen, and vibrant outdoor spaces
perfect for Coachella, Stagecoach, and desert getaways. Walking distance to
Old Town La Quinta dining. Book direct at indigopalm.co
```

**Keywords to include**:
- Coachella
- Stagecoach
- Desert getaway
- Heated pool
- Old Town La Quinta
- Vacation rental
- Private pool

### Step 6: Add Services

Create service listings (shows up in search):
- **Weekend Getaways** - $275+/night
- **Festival Stays** (Coachella/Stagecoach) - $400+/night
- **Monthly Rentals** - Contact for rates
- **Event Hosting** [if you allow]

### Step 7: Enable Messaging

Turn on Google Messages so potential guests can:
- Ask questions directly
- Get instant responses
- Convert faster than going through Airbnb

### Step 8: Get Reviews

**Critical**: Reviews boost ranking significantly

Ask past 5-star Airbnb guests:
```
"Hi [Name]! So glad you enjoyed Casa Moto. Would you mind
leaving a quick Google review? It really helps us as a
small business: [your Google Business link]

Thank you! 🌴"
```

**Target**: Get 5-10 reviews in first month

### Step 9: Add Booking Button

Once verified:
1. Go to profile dashboard
2. Click "Info" > "Add booking link"
3. Enter: https://indigopalm.co/casa-moto.html
4. This creates a "Book" button on your listing

### Step 10: Add Q&A

**Q**: "How far from Coachella?"

**Q**: "What's check-in time?"

Based on similar vacation rentals:

## Red Flags to Avoid

❌ Don't use your personal name as business name
❌ Don't stuff keywords unnaturally
❌ Don't use low-quality photos
❌ Don't create multiple listings for same property
❌ Don't buy fake reviews (Google detects this)

## Questions?

- Can't find verification option? Make sure address is exact
- Business already exists? You may need to "claim" instead of create
- Not showing in Maps? Takes 2-3 days after verification
- Low ranking? Need more reviews + complete profile
